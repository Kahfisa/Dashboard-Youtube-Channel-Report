# Dashboard-Youtube-Channel-Report

## Introduction
This project presents an interactive dashboard that analyzes YouTube channel performance across key audience engagement metrics. It tracks viewer behavior through watch time, view counts, likes, dislikes, shares, comments, and subscription activity, while also examining geolocation insights to understand audience distribution.


## Tools
- Looker Studio
  

## Business Question
- What is the total number of viewers on this YouTube channel?
- How many users have shared videos from this YouTube channel?
- How many comments have been received across all videos on this YouTube channel?
- What is the average watch duration for the videos on this YouTube channel?
- What is the trend of viewer growth on this YouTube channel over time?
- From which countries do the viewers of this YouTube channel originate?
- How many users have given likes to the videos on this YouTube channel?
- How many users have given dislikes to the videos on this YouTube channel?
- How many users have subscribed to this YouTube channel?

## Insight
- The trend in the number of viewers on the YouTube channel shows a fairly fluctuating pattern throughout the observation period. The highest view counts occurred at the end of the month, specifically on November 22, 2025 and October 31, 2025, with 997 and 977 viewers respectively.
- The audience distribution spans several continents, including the Americas, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Oceania. However, viewer contributions are not evenly distributed across all countries within each continent. For example, in Africa, only Cameroon is recorded as a source of viewership. The largest number of viewers comes from the United States, totaling 10,000 viewers. With the high viewership from the United States, efforts to maintain and increase engagement from that country can include creating content that is more relevant to American audiences and scheduling video uploads to align with U.S. time zones.
